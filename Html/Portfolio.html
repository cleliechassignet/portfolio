Frieder Nake

Processing - 2018

The aim here was to create a processing code generating random works by trying to get as close as possible to the initial works of the artists Manfred Mohr and Frieder Nake. The random process of this art prevents us from the exact copy of their work. I then wanted to integrate the artist Mondrian in the code generating a work close to Nake's one. The interest here is the crossing and adaptation of two works between them.

Eldorado cinema

3D - 2019

Realization of a 50s video in 3D with the blender software.

In pairs, we modeled the facade and a room of the art cinema Eldorado in Dijon. We have faithfully reproduced the room from the plan of the cinema, working on two atmospheres: the room on and the room off with only the projection line as light. We had to integrate and animate the cameras and add a sound atmosphere in order to create an animation. The video ends with the projection of the film "Mademoiselle" by Park Chan-Wook.


Mechanical arm

Motion design - 2019

I learned the basics of motion design by making this short video with the after effect software. The goal here was to familiarize myself with the notions of masks, expressions, relatives and many other features to keep a fluidity in the animation of 2D objects. So I created and animated a mechanical arm. I then planted a scenery, illustrated entirely on after effect, which brings a soft and delicate character to contrast with the unnatural aspect of the automaton arm.


"A croquer"

Video - 2018

A croquer is a short film that I can sum up as poetic drama. There are three distinct atmospheres shown by three different colorimetries. This film allowed me to concentrate more on the image, namely its composition, colours and framing. The intensity of the story is also based on Schubert's classical music.


Preljocaj

Motion design - 2019

I created a 40-second teaser to bring the viewer to discover the contemporary dance choreographer Angelin Preljocaj. This video includes two scenes : One of them takes a scene from his show "Les Nuits". The other takes up the advertising poster of the dance school of Preljocaj, namely "Pavillon noir". The soundtrack is taken from another work by the choreographer. It correctly represents the universe of contemporary dance by revealing the elementary and animal side of this art.
